About Me Turning ideas into functional and user-friendly web applications

Get to know me!

Hey! I'm a third-year Computer Science student at Cavite State University with a strong passion for web development and software engineering. I enjoy turning ideas into functional and user-friendly digital solutions. My technical skills include HTML, CSS, SCSS, JavaScript, React, PHP, Python, MySQL, and MongoDB, along with experience in frameworks like Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, and Laravel. I’m always eager to learn new technologies and improve my problem-solving abilities through hands-on projects. Family Background A little insight into my family and upbringing.

I come from a humble and close-knit family of six, consisting of my father, my late mother, and us four siblings.
