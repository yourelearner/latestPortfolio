Challenges and Learnings A reflection on the obstacles I've faced and the lessons I've learned.

Coping with the Loss of My Mother

Losing my mother at the age of 16 was a deeply emotional and life-changing experience.

It taught me the importance of emotional strength, independence, and staying focused despite personal hardships.

Balancing Academics and Projects

Managing time between academic responsibilities and personal projects was a significant challenge.

I learned the importance of prioritization, effective time management, and setting realistic goals to achieve balance.

Handling Group Project Challenges

Working with teams that had conflicting schedules and communication gaps made collaboration difficult.

I learned to step up as a communicator, resolve conflicts, and adapt to different working styles to ensure project success.
